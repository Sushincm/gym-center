Common Questions

What are your operating hours?
We are open from 5:00 AM to 11:00 PM on weekdays, and 6:00 AM to 9:00 PM on weekends.

Yes, we have elite certified trainers available for 1-on-1 sessions. Our 4:1 small group training is also included in select memberships.

Absolutely. We have dedicated parking spots for members right in front of the facility at Marine Drive.
